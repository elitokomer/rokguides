The most important Epic option to cover Cavalry's lack of area damage.

## Strengths

His strength is an area-damage skill that can hit up to five targets, plus the slow effects he grants when disengaging from combat or chasing down enemies.

## Weaknesses

His weakness is being extremely weak defensively, with low survivability.

## Best Pairings

He can be used with Pelagius or Minamoto. As an unusual meta pick, he can even produce surprising synergy leading a pure infantry army, purely for the slow and area-damage advantage, alongside Sun Tzu Prime.

## Talent Tree Priority

The Cavalry and Skill trees should be prioritized.

## Usage Scenario

Running hit-and-run tactics with cavalry armies in large field battles to harass enemy lines from behind.
