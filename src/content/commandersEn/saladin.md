An unshakable leader who fundamentally transforms cavalry's fragile, delicate nature, giving it the tankiness it deserves.

## Strengths

His strength is the outstandingly high defense stats he grants to cavalry troops and his resistance to counterattacks. His active skill also cuts the enemy's movement speed by thirty percent while blocking forty percent of the healing they receive. This makes him the exact antithesis of constantly self-healing commanders like Richard or Cao Cao on the field.

## Weaknesses

His weakness is that while a talent level of five-five-five-one (5-5-5-1) is plenty sufficient, he is not a pure burst-damage monster; he leans more toward control and sabotage.

## Best Pairings

Saladin + YSG (balanced area damage and high survivability) or Saladin + Minamoto (aggressive single-target damage) stand out.

## Talent Tree Priority

Defensive paths in the Cavalry tree should be chosen along with skill-speed nodes in the Support tree.

## Usage Scenario

Slowing down and catching high-priority targets trying to flee in the open field, and locking down enemy tanks' healing cycles.
