Dominating the infantry meta the moment KvK 2 begins, Alexander the Great is one of the game's most iconic characters.

## Strengths

Chief among his strengths is his active skill, which simultaneously creates a massive shield for himself while also protecting the lowest-health allied troop nearby. The enormous attack bonuses and high map movement speed he gains while shielded completely erase infantry's usual clunkiness.

## Weaknesses

His weakness is the lack of a direct area damage (AoE) ability, working entirely on a single-target basis.

## Best Pairings

Alexander + YSG is the gold standard for KvK 2 open-field battles, combining shield protection with YSG's circular damage. For defensive purposes, Charles Martel + Alexander is also a very strong alternative.

## Talent Tree Priority

Speed and defense nodes in the Infantry tree should be combined with flat-damage paths in the Attack tree.

## Usage Scenario

Using high mobility in the open field to crash into enemy archers, and distributing shields to allies in large battles to prevent the front line from breaking.
