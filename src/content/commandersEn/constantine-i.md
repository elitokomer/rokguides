A selfless support tank entirely focused on kingdom defense and banner protection.

## Strengths

His strength is a massive defense bonus that kicks in when his army's health drops below fifty percent, plus an active area support that reduces the skill damage allies take.

## Weaknesses

His weakness is very low map movement speed and almost no damage output; wandering alone in the open field, he's simply a waste of time for enemies.

## Best Pairings

His best pairings are Charles Martel for garrison defense, or Joan of Arc for open-field support.

## Talent Tree Priority

The Garrison or Defense trees should be built first.

## Usage Scenario

Stubbornly defending strategically important passes and kingdom banners against enemy rallies throughout KvK 2.
