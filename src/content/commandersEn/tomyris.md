A supporting legendary designed to cover Edward's back and leave sneaky poison stacks on the enemy.

## Strengths

Her strength is the poison stacks she passively accumulates on the enemy; these can stack up to ten times, and when her active skill triggers, they dramatically increase the skill damage the enemy takes.

## Weaknesses

Her weakness is that she has no presence at all as a standalone primary commander and is destined to be used purely as a secondary.

## Best Pairings

Edward + Tomyris or YSG + Tomyris.

## Talent Tree Priority

Since she isn't used as primary, her talent tree doesn't matter much, but if necessary lean toward Archer-focused nodes.

## Usage Scenario

Stacking poison on the enemy during open-field fights or long banner rallies to multiply the damage allies deal.
