The number-one rally and open-field weapon for high-spending (P2W) archer players during KvK 2.

## Strengths

His strength is having one of the highest single-target skill damage multipliers available at this stage of the game, and the huge boost he gives to archer troops' health and damage.

## Weaknesses

His weakness is that his active skill requires an extremely high rage cost of thirteen hundred fifty (1350). This slows down his skill cast rate and makes him quick to be targeted and punished by enemy cavalry in the open field.

## Best Pairings

His best pairing is definitely a specialized YSG placed behind him; Edward hits the single target up front while YSG clears the surrounding area from behind.

## Talent Tree Priority

Anything that increases rage generation in the Archer and Skill trees should be taken.

## Usage Scenario

Launching devastating rallies against enemy banners and cities, or melting single targets from a safe position in the back during open-field battles.
