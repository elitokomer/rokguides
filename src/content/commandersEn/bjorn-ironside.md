Sun Tzu's most loyal complement, Bjorn offers an aggressive playstyle for early-game infantry players.

## Strengths

His strength is that his active skill deals direct damage to the enemy while also increasing the skill damage taken by the targeted troop by ten percent. This debuff multiplies the damage of the commander placed behind him.

## Weaknesses

His weakness is the lack of area damage and being entirely focused on a single target.

## Best Pairings

His best pairing is definitely the combo of placing Sun Tzu behind him.

## Talent Tree Priority

Since he'll be the primary commander instead of the secondary, the Infantry and Attack trees should be prioritized.

## Usage Scenario

Leading Sun Tzu in KvK 1 open-field battles to melt down enemy targets one by one.
