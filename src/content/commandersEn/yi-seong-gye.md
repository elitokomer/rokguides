In this first and most critical phase of the Kingdoms War, YSG is the most future-proof investment in the game. Even once his expertise skill is completed, he continues to hold his power in later stages.

## Strengths

Chief among his strengths are the shield advantage and area damage he gains once his expertise skill is completed. His durability and damage balance in the open field make him one of the most reliable commanders of KvK 1.

## Talent Tree Priority

The Archer and Defense trees should be prioritized. If defending a city or banner, points can be allocated to the Garrison tree.

## Usage Scenario

Holding the front line with a shield advantage in the open field, or withstanding rallies launched against kingdom banners.

## Best Pairings

He provides balanced defense and area damage behind Saladin; with Minamoto no Yoshitsune he forms aggressive single-target combinations.
