Recruited entirely for free from Expedition mode, this legendary leader is a pure strategic support character.

## Strengths

Her main strength is reducing the attack, defense, and health of up to five enemy troops in a wide arc in front of her by a massive thirty percent all at once (debuff).

## Weaknesses

Her weakness is that her raw damage output alone is very low and her defense is fragile.

## Best Pairings

She performs excellently when Sun Tzu or Joan of Arc are placed behind her.

## Talent Tree Priority

The Leadership and Support trees should be prioritized in her talent tree.

## Usage Scenario

To trigger her abilities, join large field battles with mixed-troop armies (containing all three troop types) to collectively weaken enemy forces.
