The undisputed peak of the Cavalry class and the most feared assassin of the open field.

## Strengths

His strengths include extraordinarily high single-target burst damage, a passive effect that breaks fifteen percent of the enemy's defense, and the massive health and defense stats he grants to cavalry units. In the open field, when surrounded by enemies, he also gains a damage-reduction bonus, making him an incredible tank for a cavalry commander.

## Weaknesses

His weakness is that he has no area damage (AoE) whatsoever, being designed entirely to lock onto a single target and erase it from the map.

## Best Pairings

Nevsky + Joan of Arc (Prime) is currently the strongest open-field meta pairing for cavalry. Alternatively, Nevsky + Huatut is also a solid choice.

## Talent Tree Priority

All attack nodes in the Cavalry tree and every strategic path that speeds up rage regeneration in the Skill tree should be taken in full.

## Usage Scenario

Spotting the enemy's high-priority but fragile targets in the open field (such as archers or glass cannons like YSG) and erasing them at lightning speed.
