The commander easiest to max out quickly (to maximum skill level) with VIP packages, especially the biggest weapon for paying players during KvK 1.

## Strengths

His strength is an incredibly high single-target burst (nuke) and the high movement speed he grants to cavalry troops.

## Weaknesses

His weakness is being completely lacking in area damage and fragile in defensive stats; he melts very quickly when caught in the middle of a crowd in the open field.

## Best Pairings

With Cao Cao for pure cavalry speed and damage, or protected more safely behind Saladin during KvK 2.

## Talent Tree Priority

Damage nodes in the Cavalry and Skill trees should be prioritized.

## Usage Scenario

Hunting weak targets wandering alone in the open field, catching fleeing archers, and running quick rallies on barbarian forts.
