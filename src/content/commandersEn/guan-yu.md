One of the oldest yet still relevant open-field leaders of the conquest era.

## Strengths

His strength is an active skill that can hit up to three targets in a wide arc in front of him, with a silence effect and outstandingly high skill damage. The silence effect delays the active skill cast time of affected enemies, disrupting their rotations.

## Weaknesses

His weakness is that to get maximum value from this skill he absolutely must be used as the primary commander, and he needs external shield support to trigger the damage boost from his passive; he cannot generate a shield on his own.

## Best Pairings

He is used with Alexander (Alex) or Leonidas for shield support. In the current meta, Guan Yu + Liu Che is also very popular.

## Talent Tree Priority

The Infantry and Skill trees should be used in balance, with the top-tier nodes that directly boost active skill damage absolutely unlocked.

## Usage Scenario

Standing at the very front line in large alliance battles to silence enemy groups en masse and cripple their skill cycles.
