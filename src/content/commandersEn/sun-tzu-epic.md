Standing toe-to-toe with legendary commanders and by far the best of all epic characters in the game, Sun Tzu is the apple of F2P players' eyes.

## Strengths

His strength is that his active skill can hit up to five targets with area damage at once, and the extra rage he gains per target hit pushes his skill cycle to insane speeds. His passive's flat twenty percent skill damage boost also delivers tremendous value.

## Weaknesses

His weakness is that, being an epic character, his base stats are low; he falls short in pure defense against legendary armies.

## Best Pairings

With Bjorn Ironside as primary and Sun Tzu as secondary, he forms a fantastic KvK 1 starting army. He also works wonders behind Charles Martel.

## Talent Tree Priority

Basic health points should be gathered from the Infantry tree, with all remaining strategic points spent maximizing rage generation in the Skill tree.

## Usage Scenario

Allowing low-budget accounts to farm report points at maximum efficiency in KvK 1 open-field battles.
