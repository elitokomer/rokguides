Descending on the enemy like a nightmare, he is one of the most insidious executioners on the battlefield.

## Strengths

His strength is a high damage multiplier along with the Malice stacks he leaves on the enemy. As the battle drags on, these stacks sabotage the opponent's defensive mechanisms, making them increasingly fragile and vulnerable.

## Weaknesses

His weakness is that while he does great work alone, he needs a fast, high rage-cycle partner who can instantly convert the vulnerabilities he creates into raw damage.

## Best Pairings

His best pairing is definitely the setup with Sun Tzu (Prime) as primary and Bai Qi as secondary.

## Talent Tree Priority

When he leads the army, focus on the synergy between the Infantry and Skill trees to balance durability and damage.

## Usage Scenario

Breaking the enemy front's defensive resistance in long attrition battles in the open field.
