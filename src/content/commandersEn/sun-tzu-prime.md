The state-of-the-art evolution of the classic Sun Tzu's famous rage-monster build, turned into a devastating force in the legendary league.

## Strengths

Chief among his strengths is offering a fast rage cycle and wide area damage in the open field. But what truly sets him apart is the Smite damage mechanic he introduces to the game and his True Damage bonuses that ignore armor entirely.

## Weaknesses

His weakness is that because he focuses so heavily on high damage potential, he doesn't carry as much pure defense as classic tank infantry commanders; if positioned incorrectly he can take heavy damage.

## Best Pairings

Standing side by side with Bai Qi, he becomes one of the most elite and unstoppable damage machines in the open field. He also works flawlessly with Liu Che or William Wallace.

## Talent Tree Priority

Since all his power comes from his skills, basic defensive elements in the Infantry tree should be locked in, with all remaining points going to nodes that accelerate rage generation in the Skill tree.

## Usage Scenario

Standing at the very front in large field battles (murderball) to collectively melt down enemy armies with bursts of true damage.
